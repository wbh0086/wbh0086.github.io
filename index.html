I am an Associate Professor at the Department of Mechanical Engineering and Maritime Studies, Western Norway University of Applied Sciences (HVL), Campus i Kronstad, Bergen, Norway.

My research interests are in scope of human factors and human-in-the-loop topics in maritime operations (specifically on maritime autonomous surface ships (MASS)) and transportation systems, by means of cybernetics theories and technologies, including artificial intelligence & machine learning, sensor fusion, data mining, GNC (guidance, navigation, and control), etc. My research interests also include fluid-structure interaction, specifically experiment-based vortex-induced vibration (VIV).
