My name is Baiheng WU
& I’m a researcher
in maritime operations

I am a post-doctoral research fellow at the Department of ICT and Natural Science (IIR), Norwegian University of Science and Technolgy (NTNU), Campus in Ålesund.

My research interests are in scope of human factors and ergonomics in maritime operations and transportation systems, by means of cybernetics theories and technologies, including artificial intelligence & machine learning, sensor fusion, data mining, GNC (guidance, navigation, and control), etc. My research interests also extend to fluid-structure interaction, specifically experiment-based vortex-induced vibration (VIV).
